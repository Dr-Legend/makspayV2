Unaddressed Market...

A street hawker, who wants to access credit to stock up her supplies; borrows money everyday from local money lenders at exorbitant interest rates.

She needs simple, affordable credit solution to meet her day to day business needs. Over 50 million people in this Informal Sector like Vimala, face the same challenge everyday!

We help people like Vimala get access to Formal Bank Credit at affordable interest rates.

Underserved Daily Wage Workers...

A Daily Wage Worker, who works for less than ₹250 per day, gets paid in T10-15 days.

She borrows money in-lieu of this payment to support her family.

She needs access to secure and affordable credit solutions to improve her livelihood.

Over 282 million people are in this segment.

We help Daily wage workers get Instant Loans against their wages.
